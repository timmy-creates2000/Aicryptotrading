BASIC CRYPTO TRADING STRATEGY

=== ENTRY RULES ===

BUY Conditions (ALL must be true):
1. RSI between 30-50 (not overbought)
2. MACD histogram turning positive
3. Price above EMA21 (uptrend)
4. Volume above average (confirmation)
5. No recent losing trades on this pair

SELL Conditions (ALL must be true):
1. RSI between 50-70 (not oversold)
2. MACD histogram turning negative
3. Price below EMA21 (downtrend)
4. Volume above average (confirmation)
5. No recent losing trades on this pair

=== EXIT RULES ===

Stop Loss: 2% below entry
Take Profit: 4% above entry (2:1 reward/risk)
Trailing Stop: Activate at +2%, trail by 1%

=== RISK MANAGEMENT ===

Max Position Size: $0.50 per trade
Max Daily Loss: 10% of account
Max Consecutive Losses: 3 (then pause)
Leverage: 2x maximum

=== MARKET CONDITIONS TO AVOID ===

DO NOT TRADE when:
- Major news events (Fed announcements, CPI data)
- Extremely low volume (< 50% of average)
- High volatility (ATR > 2x normal)
- Weekend trading (Sat-Sun)
- First/last hour of trading day

=== TIMEFRAMES ===

Primary: 15-minute charts
Confirmation: Check 1-hour trend
Avoid: Trades against 1-hour trend

=== PAIRS TO FOCUS ===

Preferred: BTC/USDT, ETH/USDT (high liquidity)
Avoid: Low volume altcoins

=== PSYCHOLOGY RULES ===

- Never revenge trade after a loss
- Stick to the plan
- Take breaks after 3 consecutive losses
- Review trades weekly
- Adjust strategy based on performance data
